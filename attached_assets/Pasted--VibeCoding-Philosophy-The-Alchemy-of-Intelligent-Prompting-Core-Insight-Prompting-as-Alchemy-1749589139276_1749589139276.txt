## Core Insight: Prompting as Alchemy

VibeCoding is built on the fundamental understanding that prompting is an **alchemy of sorts** - the intelligent combination of human language versatility with the structure of command-line syntax and code.

### The Power Level Principle

**Each word has a power level** - this is the foundational truth behind effective AI collaboration:

- **High-Power Words**: Action verbs, specific nouns, technical terms with precise meaning
